# PEAK-System Technik

PEAK-System Technik from Darmstadt, Germany is a leading provider of hardware, software, and services for the automotive and industrial communication sector with emphasis on the field buses CAN and LIN. The product range includes:

## Hardware ...

- CAN FD connections for High-speed USB 2.0, PCI Express, PCI Express Mini, and M.2
- CAN/LIN interfaces for conventional PC interfaces and embedded applications
- I/O modules with CAN connection for control, measured data recording, and processing
- Converters for different physical transmission types (bus converter modules)
- Routers and gateways for forwarding messages between CAN buses and other networks
- Data loggers and diagnostic hardware
- Products for education, demonstrations, and test setups
- Chip solutions for the CAN and CAN FD connection to USB and PCI Express
 
## Software ...

- CAN development systems for Windows® and Linux
- Programming interfaces for various protocols and standards
- Software to monitor and diagnose CAN and LIN buses
- Programs for recording, playback, and simulation of message traffic
- Configuration software for CAN hardware from PEAK-System
 
## Accessories ...

- Helpful CAN accessories
- PC adapter cards for PC/104 Small Form Factor Boards
- CAN and adapter cables for various applications
 
Ngoài việc phát triển và phân phối các sản phẩm phần cứng và phần mềm, **PEAK-System** còn cung cấp giải pháp dưới dạng các dịch vụ khác nhau.

## History

`1999`: PEAK-System Technik GmbH is founded with Mr. Uwe Wilhelm as CEO
`2003`: Thành lập công ty con độc lập tại France
`2012`: In addition to Mr. Wilhelm, the management of PEAK-System Technik GmbH is expanded with Mr. Alexander Gach
`2014`: Successful certification according to the international standards ISO 9001 and ISO 14001
`2024`: PEAK-System Technik GmbH is acquired by HMS Networks. Mr. Thomas Conz takes over the operational management of the company
